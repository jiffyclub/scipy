scipy.special.zeta¶

scipy.special.zeta(x, q=None, out=None)[source]¶

Riemann zeta function.

The two-argument version is the Hurwitz zeta function:

\[\zeta(x, q) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{(k + q)^x},\]

Riemann zeta function corresponds to q = 1.
