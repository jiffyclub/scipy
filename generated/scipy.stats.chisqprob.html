scipy.stats.chisqprob¶

scipy.stats.chisqprob(*args, **kwds)[source]¶

chisqprob is deprecated! stats.chisqprob is deprecated in scipy 0.17.0; use stats.distributions.chi2.sf instead.

Probability value (1-tail) for the Chi^2 probability distribution.

Broadcasting rules apply.

Parameters:

chisq : array_like or float > 0

df : array_like or float, probably int >= 1
Returns:

chisqprob : ndarray

The area from chisq to infinity under the Chi^2 probability distribution with degrees of freedom df.
